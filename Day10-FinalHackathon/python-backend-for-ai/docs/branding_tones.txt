Playful Brand:

Tone: Friendly, casual

Colors: Pastels, soft hues (light pink, mint green)

Fonts: Rounded sans-serif (e.g., Poppins, Quicksand)

Layout: Lots of white space, emojis/icons, curved corners

Professional Brand:

Tone: Confident, reliable

Colors: Navy, maroon, white, charcoal

Fonts: Serif or clean sans-serif (e.g., Roboto, Lora)

Layout: Grid-based, minimal animations, clear hierarchy

Luxury Brand:

Tone: Exclusive, elegant

Colors: Black, gold, ivory, deep blue

Fonts: High-contrast serif (e.g., Playfair Display)

Layout: Spacious, focused on typography and imagery
